Virtual "Cry Companion"

Phone-A-Friend

Are you a current or former NSS student who has fallen into the pit of despair and found yourself crying in the shower? Have you since graduated from the program and STILL find yourself crying in the shower? Great... this position is for you!

Requirements

  • Applicant muste be a NSS student or NSS alumni
  • Applicant must have the ability to show compassion and sympathy for those who are in the valley of despair
  • Applicant must be non-judgmental and have the ability to listen....JUST LISTEN!
  • Applicant must have internet connection and a working phone line.

Customer Support "Cry Companion"

Customer Support Technician

Requirements

  • Applicant must have customer service experience
  • Applicant must have strong problem solving skills, i.e. Javscript experience is not required, but helpful!
  • Applicant must know when to shut up and listen-- advice is not always welcomed.
